About ME

My name is Junlong Zhang. I am a senior at MSU, majoring in Games & Interactive Media. I like playing games, listening to music, and playing drums. My favorite game genres are FPS, RPG, and TBS. Additionally, I enjoy studying the connection between game story and gameplay, as well as the relationship between characters in the story and the player's reaction/interaction with the game characters. This website showcases my works and provides my contact information. Thank you for reading this far!
